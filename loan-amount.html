About Nyota Fund

Nyota Fund is a digital micro-finance platform providing fast, transparent, and accessible short-term loans across Kenya. Our goal is to make borrowing simple and convenient for individuals who need quick financial support for emergencies, business boosts, school needs, or personal expenses.

We value transparency, efficiency, and accessibility — no collateral, no guarantor, and no long paperwork. All fees are clearly displayed before approval.

Once your processing fee is verified, funds are sent instantly to your M-Pesa. Verification may take up to 48 hours depending on system queue.
