About Nyota Fund

Partner with us

Nyota Fund supports youth and community initiatives by providing scholarships, microgrants and mentorship. Our selection processes are community-driven and our work focuses on measurable outcomes, sustainability and capacity building.

Mission

To expand opportunities for Kenyan youth by funding education, enterprise and leadership development that create long-term value.

Approach

  • Community-led selection and local partnerships
  • Transparent budgeting, auditing and public reporting
  • Post-award monitoring, technical support and mentorship
